Why It Matters

Unit 3 provides you with an in-depth study of the skeletal (Chapters 7 & 8) and muscular (Chapter 9) systems. You will really be using the anatomic terminology you learned in Chapter 1. You will apply what you're learning to understand how the bones protect us, provide structural support, store chemicals like calcium and phosphate, and host the production of blood.

You'll also apply what you've learned about cells and tissues to understand the unique functions of the muscular system and how this system provides movement, protection, and support. These two systems work together to give us both stability and movement. Enjoy learning about your amazing body!

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to demonstrate competency in the following:

1

Recognize major structures of the skeletal and muscular systems.

2

Explain how structure and function are related in these systems to support body function.

How to Navigate This Module

Be sure to complete the assignments in the order listed! This module covers three chapters and three labs. Begin each chapter by reading through that chapter on your own using the digital e-text or, if you prefer, the optional hard copy you purchased. Once you are familiar with the chapter, you can complete the assignments more effectively.

Within each module, you will find an interactive reading assignment and quiz over each chapter, three lab exercises (some labs have two parts) and three lab quizzes, one Case Study assignment, one Lecture Exam, and one Lab Exam. Some modules also include videos to support your studies.
