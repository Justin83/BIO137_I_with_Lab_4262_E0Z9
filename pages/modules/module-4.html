Why It Matters

You will be spending all of Unit 4 getting to know the nervous system! From the unique features of individual neurons to the organization of the brain and nerves, you will learn how this extraordinary system helps coordinate body functions from reflexes to complex decision making.

In Chapter 10 you'll begin by learning the basic structures and functions of the nervous system. You will build on that in Chapter 11 by learning how the system is divided into distinct anatomic and functional divisions. Finally, you'll conclude your study of the nervous system by learning how the system allows us to experience and respond to our environment through our senses. Enjoy this last adventure in your journey of discovering the anatomy and physiology of the human body!

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to demonstrate competency in the following:

1

Recognize major structures of the nervous system.

2

Explain how structure and function are related in this system to support body function.
