Why It Matters

You've made it to the final stretch! This module pulls together everything you have studied this semester — all 12 lecture chapters and all 12 lab activities. The comprehensive final is your opportunity to demonstrate how much you have learned about the structure and function of the human body.

The final review quizzes are designed to help you revisit each module and prepare with confidence. Work through them carefully, revisit the chapters and labs where you feel less sure, and reach out if you have questions. Finish strong!

How to Prepare

Start with the final review quizzes — they are organized by module so you can pinpoint the areas that need the most attention. As you review, return to the chapters, SmartBook exercises, and lab activities for any topics you find challenging.

Plan your time within the exam window so you can complete both the lecture and lab portions without rushing, and make sure you have a reliable internet connection before you begin. See the schedule in Blackboard for all official dates and times.
