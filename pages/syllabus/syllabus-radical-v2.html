Weekly Work

The pace is steady: three to five assignments every 2-3 days.

Reading and SmartBook

Read each chapter and complete the Interactive SmartBook Exercise. SmartBook assignments have no time limit and may be repeated before the due date.

Practice Quizzes

Chapter practice quizzes are open-book/open-note, limited to 30 minutes, and may be repeated up to 3 times. The best score is used.

Labs and Case Studies

Lab exercises may be repeated before the due date. Lab quizzes are limited to 30 minutes and may be repeated up to 3 times. Four case studies are submitted in Blackboard.

Exams

Four module exams and one comprehensive final exam.

Module Exams

  • 4 module exams.
  • Each module includes separate lecture and lab exams.
  • Lecture: 30 questions, 60 points, 60 minutes.
  • Lab: 20 questions, 40 points, 40 minutes.

Final Exam

  • Comprehensive over all 12 lecture chapters and 12 labs.
  • Lecture: 60 questions, 60 points, 120 minutes.
  • Lab: 40 questions, 40 points, 80 minutes.

Final Review

Final review quizzes are divided by module and lecture/lab content and are worth 20 points total.

Make-up exam note: Students who miss a module exam may be eligible for one make-up exam opportunity near the end of the semester. Information is provided during the final weeks. This does not apply to the final exam.

Late Work and Policies

Use the built-in flexibility wisely.

Late Assignments

Most assignments include an automatic two-day extension beyond the original due date. Students may continue working during this extension without contacting the instructor.

After the Extension

After the late submission deadline passes, the assignment closes and no additional submissions are accepted. Individual reopen requests are generally not granted.

Withdrawal

Withdrawal requests after midterm are at instructor discretion and require continued course participation and at least 80% of coursework to that date completed with a grade more than 0.
