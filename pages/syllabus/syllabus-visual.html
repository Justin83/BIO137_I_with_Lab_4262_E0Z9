Weekly Rhythm

Three to five assignments are due every 2-3 days every week of the semester, including exam weeks, holidays, and breaks.

1. Read and Practice

Read each chapter, complete SmartBook, then complete the chapter practice quiz.

2. Apply and Lab

Complete case studies when assigned, lab exercises, and lab quizzes.

3. Prepare for Exams

Module exams occur at the end of each 3-chapter module and include separate lecture and lab parts.

6

Exams and Grading

Module Exams

  • 4 module exams total.
  • Each module has a lecture exam and a lab exam.
  • Lecture portion: 60 points.
  • Lab portion: 40 points.
  • Combined module value: 100 points.

Final Exam

  • Comprehensive final over all 12 chapters and 12 labs.
  • Lecture portion: 60 points.
  • Lab portion: 40 points.
  • Total final exam value: 100 points.
Make-up exam note: Students who miss a module exam may be eligible for one make-up exam opportunity near the end of the semester. This does not apply to the final exam.
